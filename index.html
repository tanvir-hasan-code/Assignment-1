The Vision of Future

The vision of Bangladesh 2.0 is to build a prosperous, inclusive, and sustainable nation driven by innovation, digital transformation, and green growth. It aims to empower its people and ensure resilience in a rapidly evolving global landscape. Bangladesh 2.0 envisions leveraging technology and innovation to create a knowledge-based economy. It aspires to position the nation as a global leader in sustainability and digital excellence.

Digital Transformation

The transformation of Bangladesh 2.0 focuses on creating a smart, inclusive, and sustainable future. It emphasizes digital innovation, modern infrastructure, and green growth to empower citizens and drive economic progress.

Green Energy

Green Energy in Bangladesh 2.0 represents a commitment to sustainable and renewable energy solutions. It emphasizes expanding solar, wind, and hydroelectric power projects to reduce dependency on fossil fuels.

Modern Infrastructure

Modern Infrastructure in Bangladesh 2.0 focuses on developing smart cities, efficient transportation systems, and robust digital connectivity. It includes sustainable urban planning, advanced public utilities, and resilient infrastructure to support economic growth.

A key aspect of Bangladesh 2.0 is the development of a thriving digital economy. This includes fostering e-commerce, digital banking, online education, telemedicine, and more. Digital platforms are expected to provide new opportunities for entrepreneurs, small businesses, and startups, contributing to job creation and economic diversification. The government is keen to promote digital payment systems and fintech innovations to ease financial transactions and improve financial inclusion across the country.In Bangladesh 2.0, cities are envisioned as smart and sustainable hubs powered by technology. Smart cities would use technology to improve urban infrastructure, reduce traffic congestion, manage waste, and promote energy efficiency. Through IoT (Internet of Things) solutions, cities can be equipped with sensors that provide real-time data for better decision-making. Dhaka, the capital, is at the center of this transformation, with plans to improve transportation, public services, and utilities using advanced technologies.

Bangladesh Launches New Satellite


Date:01/11/2025 Time:10:25 PM

Bangladesh has made significant strides in space technology with the launch of its first satellite, the Bangabandhu Satellite-1, on May 12, 2018. This geostationary communications and broadcasting satellite was named after the nation's founding leader, Bangabandhu Sheikh Mujibur Rahman. Manufactured by Thales Alenia Space, it was launched aboard a SpaceX Falcon 9 Block 5 rocket from Kennedy Space Center in the United States. With this launch, Bangladesh became the 57th country to independently operate a satellite in space.
